# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

AI Cloud Study Saver helps students create flashcards from study notes and track learning progress.

## Information Collected

The app may collect:

- Student login ID and display name.
- Uploaded or pasted study notes.
- Generated flashcards.
- Flashcard ratings such as Strong, Review, and Weak.
- Saved study session dates and progress data.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Create and save flashcards.
- Show study progress and saved sessions.
- Support admin monitoring of learning activity.
- Improve the study workflow inside the app.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses:

- Firebase for account and study session storage.
- Groq for AI flashcard generation.
- Render for hosting the app.

Study notes may be sent to the AI provider when flashcards are generated.

## Data Security

Student passwords are stored as salted password hashes. API keys, Firebase credentials, and deployment secrets are stored outside the public repository.

## Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of account and study data by contacting the app owner.

## Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the app owner.
